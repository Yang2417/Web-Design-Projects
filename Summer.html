History

Summer was the great-grandchild of Pekingese immigrants to the Philippines. Summer was born to Roxy, a single-mom, on her mother's house in Las Pinas on early 2021, the second of three pups. Her siblings are Chimmy (b.2021) and Snow (b.2021). At 7 months old, Summer left on her own to pursue an independent lifestyle. She worked as a professional cuddler and a guard dog at the same time. She learned tricks, games, and commands (mostly, sit) through self-study

At 10 months old, she reunited with her mother and sibling Snow. However, her sibling is indifferent from her affections and this led to an altercation. Until now, no lawsuit was filed for this incident.
